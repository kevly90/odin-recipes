Pumpkin Juice

This is a staple at the Hogwarts Great Hall. This recipe is taken from Favorite Family Recipes

Ingredients

Serves 1
  • 4 liters apple cider or apple juice
  • 5 ounces pumpkin puree
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 2 teaspoons pumpkin pie spice
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients in a large pitcher and stir well
  2. Pour over ice in individual glasses, and top with cinnamon sticks and a straw. (Apple slices optional)
  3. Stir again before each serving to prevent settling.
